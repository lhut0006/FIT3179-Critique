Tweets around the world

This choropleth map visualizes the global engagement with the hashtag #trump on Twitter during the 2020 U.S. presidential election. The colors represent an Engagement Index, using min-max normalization to show the relative number of tweets per capita across countries.

Unsurprisingly, North America, particularly the United States and Canada, show the highest levels of engagement. Other regions, such as Australia and parts of Europe, also demonstrate significant interest in the election.

In contrast, large areas in Africa, South America, and parts of Asia show minimal engagement, highlighting how attention to the U.S. election varied globally.
